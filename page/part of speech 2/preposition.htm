Preposition is consist by two word there are pre (before) and position. It is mean that preposition is word for describe location and time.

There's two categorize of prepostion, place and time.

  1. Preposition of place
    • ON
      On is used when it's line surface like a river
    • IN
      IN used when it's a larger area like city, country,
    • AT
      At an area or event where there is a group of people
  2. Preposition of time
    • ON
      ON used when we talk about days, special days, or particular of a day
    • IN
      IN used when we talk about a longer time periods as season like week, month, years
    • AT
      AT used when we talk about a point of time : night and time. It also be used for short periods : the end of january, the beginning of the years
