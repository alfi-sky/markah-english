Present Perfect Continuous tense
Present perfect continuous tense berguna untuk menyatakan durasi dari peristiwa (indefinite past) yang masih berlangsung hingga saat ini
I have been making my green technology for eight years
S + have/has + been + verb-ing + for/since + amount of time/start time
Questions in present perfect continuous tense
How long+ have/has+ S + been ver-ing
How long have you been writing this book ?
