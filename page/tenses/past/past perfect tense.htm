Past Perfect Tense
Past Perfect Tense digunakan untuk menjelaskan dua atau lebih kegiatan yang telah selesai dibanding suatu kegiatan tertentu di masa lalu .
Kita pakai Past perfect untuk menyatakan peristiwa yang lebih dulu dengan waktu yang tidak tentu
Simple past clause + S + had + verb 3
I built learning website last month, I had built it twice before.

Part Perfect Tense Questions

  • Interogative 1
    Where had you been ?
    Interogative + had + S + verb 3
  • Interogative 2
    What had you called when I called you last night ?
    Interogative + had + S + verb 3 + simple past clause
  • Short answer question 1
    Had you gotten your laptop before you wrote your novel last night ?
    Had + S + verb 3 + Simple past clause
  • Short answer question 2
    Usually, it's used in coversation like;
    A : I went to my uncle's mansion last night.
    B : Have you been there before ?
