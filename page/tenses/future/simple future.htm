Simple Future Tense
Simple future tense digunakan untuk menyatakan future plans
I will rule the world one day
S + will + verb 1
Cara lain membentuk simple future tense is like this:
S + to be + going to + verb 1
Will
we will play badminton monday
going to
I'am going to Bulukumba (future), because My Mother's official duties was moved on there
