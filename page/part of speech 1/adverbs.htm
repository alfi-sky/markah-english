Adverb
Adverbs ^adverb terdiri dari dua kata yakni ad (to/menyesuaikan) dan verb (kata kerja) artinya adverb menyesuaikan gambaran/realitas kerjaan^ menjelaskan verb, adjective, atau adverbs yang lain.
Penjelaskan sederhananya adverbs biasanya disebut keterangan dalam kalimat
Sapril is slow (adjective) ^disini slow menjadi adjective karena mendeskripsikan tentang subject^
Sapril is slowly (adverb) ^disini slowly menjadi adverbs karena menjelaskan verbs. itulah sebabnya (biasanya) adverbs justru berakhiran dengan -ly^

Adverbs terbagi menjadi beberapa :

  1. Adverbs of Sequence ~ keterangan urutan kerjaan
    First,Sakina have been graduated fastly
    Two, Sakina have been admitted her portofolio to LPDP
    Third, Sakina got his letter of acceptance (LoA) at the beginning of December 2021
  2. Adverbs of frequency ~ keterangan seberapa sering kerjaan dilakukan :
    Hikari always reads book
  3. Adverbs of time ~ Keterangan waktu :
    Salmah will play badminton tomorrow
  4. Adverb yang menjelaskan tentang adverb
    Ahmad is really good people
